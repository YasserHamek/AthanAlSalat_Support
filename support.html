Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

To restore your previous purchases, go to the Settings tab in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

How do I set my location?

The app requests your GPS location automatically to calculate accurate prayer times. If you prefer a manual location, tap the location card at the top of the Prayer screen to switch to a city search.

Why do my prayer times differ from other apps?

Prayer time calculations depend on the calculation method used. Different methods yield slightly different times. You can change the method in Settings > Calculation Method to match your local authority or preference.

How do I enable or change the athan sound?

Tap any prayer card on the Prayer screen, then tap the sound icon. You can choose between None, Silent, Beep, or Athan. If you choose Athan, you can then select a sound and apply it to one prayer or all prayers.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings > Language. أذان الصلاة: Athan al Salat has its own built-in language setting and supports 76 languages. It does not follow your device language automatically.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Please email us at contact@yasserhamek.com with a description of the issue or suggestion. Include your device model and iOS version when reporting a bug.
